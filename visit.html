Plan Your Adventure

Location: Eastern South Sudan, near the Ethiopian border

Best Time to Visit: December to April (dry season)

Park Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM daily

Park Entry Fees

Category Fee (USD)
Citizens $5
Foreign Visitors $25
Students $2
Children Under 5 Free

Travel Tips

  • Hire a local guide for the best experience.
  • Wear neutral colors and carry plenty of water.
  • Respect wildlife and avoid feeding animals.
