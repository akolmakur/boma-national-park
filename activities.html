Things to Do at Boma

Explore Boma National Park through a variety of activities designed to bring you closer to nature and culture:

Wildlife Safari

Guided vehicle tours through vast savannas to witness incredible wildlife migrations.

Walking Trails

Experience the park on foot with trained rangers and immerse yourself in its natural beauty.

Capture stunning landscapes and unique wildlife moments.

Camping

Sleep under the stars at designated campsites for an authentic wilderness experience.

Cultural Tours
